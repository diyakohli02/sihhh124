Government Schemes & Incentives

Access financial support and incentives for your RTRWH project.

National Schemes

Jal Shakti Abhiyan: Catch The Rain

National campaign promoting water conservation and rainwater harvesting across all districts.

Coverage: Pan-India

Atal Bhujal Yojana

Sustainable groundwater management with a focus on community participation in water-stressed areas.

States: Gujarat, Haryana, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh

State-Specific Programs

CHHATA (Odisha)

Community Harnessing and Harvesting Rainwater Artificially from Terrace to Aquifer.

Subsidy: Up to 50% (₹55,000 max)

Mukhyamantri Jal Swavalamban Abhiyan (Rajasthan)

A comprehensive water conservation program with RWH components across all districts.

Coverage: All districts of Rajasthan

Jalyukta Shivar (Maharashtra)

Village-level water conservation with individual RWH support to make villages drought-free.

Target: Make villages drought-free
